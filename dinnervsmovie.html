    Dinner vs. Movies

    Which industry bounced back better after COVID-19?

    In this section, we began the project by using three data sets of market data to analyze the full-service restaurant industry and the movie theater industry. We specifically looked at how each industry performed in the years leading up to the pandemic and how they recovered in the following years.

    The image to the left will be our visualization for the market analysis of the U.S. movie theater industry. The data shows consumer expenditure at movie theaters in the U.S. over a span of about 10 years.

    The image to the right will be replaced with our visualization for the market analysis of the full-service restaurant industry. The data shows how the market was affected by the pandemic over the same interval as the movie theater industry.

    The image above will be our visualization that compares the two industries by percent change in each industry. This text box will be used to convey our insights from this visualization.
